Application process

  • read the guidelines and the rules.
  • complete the form below.
  • apply to how many subprojects you want, but please complete a separate form for each of them.
  • let us get to know you by telling us why you chose that particular subproject, what motivated you to apply and why do you think you are the right person for the job.
  • describe the way you would solve the task and how you would divide your time to solve it.
  • all organizers, mentors and partners will have access to your application form.
  • mentors will evaluate your application looking for dedication and willingness to learn new things and may call you to schedule an interview.

Tips and tricks

  • contact the mentors today.
  • download and compile the source code.
  • try to submit a patch/fix a bug.
  • use the projects and ROSEdu's communities (IRC, mailing lists).
  • ask any questions you have on our rsoc-community mailing list.
